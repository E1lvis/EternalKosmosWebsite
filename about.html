About Us




Founder

Ollie Meeks III

Hi, my name is Ollie Meeks III and I am one half ownership of Eternal Kosmos Inc. I graduated from West Mecklenburg High School in 2018 and had full focus on IT and Software development. I spent most of my high school time learning C# and building PCs. I got my very first job at Harris Teeter for the soul purpose of building a gaming computer. I saved up $800 and got to work. Ever since then I learned more and more about IT and building computers. It became a passion and my computer is one of the most important things to me. My goal is to make this company a brand and help gamers get the PC of their dreams. More so, get ANYONE the PC of their dreams. I am fully self taught in programming and my IT knowledge comes from building, fixing and upgrading my own PC over the past 6-8 years. I am currently working towards getting my CompTIA Certification in 2023 to expand my knowledge and be able to offer you as many IT services as possible. Thank you for giving us a chance and I hope to be able to build you a PC.
